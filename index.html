Ipser Labs — B2B onboarding delivery cadence

  • Supported 5 onboardings (often 2 in parallel); contributed to 4 on-time go-lives.
  • Requirements workshops + mapping sheets + UAT checklist → Jira execution.
  • Enablement docs + trainings reduced repeat questions (5/week → 2/week).

Lean PDCA pilot — checkout delays

  • Time study (60 customers) identified payment as bottleneck.
  • Countermeasures: standard work, 6S layout, express lane, error-proofing.
  • Results: overrides 12% → 8%, lead time 5.5 → 5.1 min.

Streamlit dashboard — demoable analytics
