Context

Campus marketplaces fail when trust and operational ownership aren‚Äôt defined. This case focuses on making the MVP launch-ready: clear scope, predictable delivery steps, and operational workflows for verification, moderation, and support.

Problem

Students needed a trusted, campus-specific way to buy/sell items. Generic marketplaces create friction around verification, safety, moderation, and support ‚Äî which leads to low adoption and poor retention.

Approach (Implementation mindset)

  • Define tight MVP scope with acceptance criteria
  • Turn scope into a delivery plan (milestones + owners)
  • Plan UAT and go-live readiness (checklists + issues)
  • Design rollout operations (support + escalation)
  • Track success with simple KPIs and a tracker

Delivery artifacts

  • Workback plan: WBS + 10 milestone schedule
  • RACI: ownership and handoffs
  • RAID: risk and dependency tracking

  • Issue capture + triage approach (severity/priority)

  • Release notes / FAQ outline for onboarding

  • Listing moderation guidelines (what gets flagged/removed)
