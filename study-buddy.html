Study Buddy Finder — Northeastern Edition

NU-only study partner matching + scheduling concept designed to create consistent study routines with trust & safety and explainable AI matching.

Product AI matching Trust & Safety Scheduling
Live Prototype

Problem

  • Schedule mismatch (classes, co-op timelines, time zones/campuses)
  • Low-signal matching (unclear compatibility: goals, study style, overlap)
  • Trust & safety concerns when meeting new people

MVP Solution

  • AI match ranking + “Why this match?” explainability
  • Overlap-based scheduling suggestions (“best overlaps”)
  • In-chat Study Coach (session plans + quizzes, with disclaimers)
  • Trust & safety: report/block from chat, privacy controls

Success metrics

  • North Star: Weekly Study Sessions Completed
  • Verification → onboarding completion
  • Match quality (match → chat → schedule conversion)
  • Time-to-first-session, session completion, “study again”
